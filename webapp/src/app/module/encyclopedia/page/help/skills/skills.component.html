Skills

Your pets will improve their skills by using them; for example, a pet that goes Fishing may learn more about Nature.

  • Arcana: Knowledge of all things magical.
  • Brawl: The ability to land - and dodge - blows in combat.
  • Crafts: Knowledge and skill to put materials together in novel ways.
  • Music: From music theory, to singing, to guitar.
  • Nature: Knowledge about the wilderness, including its flora and fauna, and where to find them.
  • Science: Technical knowledge and skill, including astronomy, and computer science.
  • Stealth: The ability to hide, and move silently.

Attributes

A pet's attributes increase only rarely. They affect a wide range of pet activities, but not as heavily as Skills do, especially for very skilled pets.

  • Strength: Raw, physical power.
  • Stamina: Endurance; toughness; health.
  • Dexterity: Motor skills, and hand-eye coordination.
  • Perception: The ability to see patterns and spot details.
  • Intelligence: Creative thinking, and puzzle-solving.
