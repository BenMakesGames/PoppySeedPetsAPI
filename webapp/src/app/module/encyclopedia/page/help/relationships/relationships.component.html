Poppyopedia » Glossary » Relationships

Pets can form a number of different types of relationships.

Rivals, Friends, Lovers...

When two pets meet - perhaps at a park event, in a band, or just by living together - they will form some kind of relationship. Relationships usually start as simple friendships, but it's possible for some pets to start off as friendly rivals, BFFs, or more.

As pets hang out, their relationship may change. It's possible that two pets will have the same goals - perhaps both would like to date - in which case things will progress well. However it's also possible that pets have different ideas about where they'd like their relationship to go. This can lead to drama, and breakups!

The relationship status of two pets may lead to different types of activities when hanging out. For example, friendly rivals meet exclusively to compete with one another, and pets that are dating may "have fun ;)" and become pregnant.

Pets with stronger relationships - BFFs, dating - will hang out more often.
