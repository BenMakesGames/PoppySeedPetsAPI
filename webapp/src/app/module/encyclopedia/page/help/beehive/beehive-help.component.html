Poppyopedia » Glossary » Beehive

The Beehive is an add-on you can get for your house. It produces several items passively in real time, but produces even more when fed with floral, fruity, and planty foods.

Flower Power

Flower Power is a kind of fuel for your beehive. It's filled by feeding the beehive, and used up by the bees to produce more and faster.

The more bees in your Beehive, the faster they work, but also the more items you'll have to feed them!

More?!

As you build up your household, the Beehive will gain new powers. You'll just have to play to find out what they are!
