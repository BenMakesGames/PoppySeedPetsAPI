Poppyopedia » Glossary » Flavors

All food items have a flavor profile - some number of flavors that describes how they taste. A food might be "floral", "tannic", "meaty", "fruity", etc.

Every pet has a flavor that they particularly enjoy. When you feed a pet food which contains their favorite flavor, the pet will feel extra loved, and gain a bit of Affection for you.
