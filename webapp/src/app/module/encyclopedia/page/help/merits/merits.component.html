Poppyopedia » Glossary » Merits

A Merit is a special ability a pet can gain. Merits vary widely in effect. Some allow your pet to wear hats (purely cosmetic), while others allow pets to engage in entirely new activities.

Merits can be acquired in a variety of ways:

  • By increasing your pet's Affection for you, for example by petting it and feeding it foods of its favorite flavor.
  • As your pet grows, it will naturally pick up a couple Merits.
  • Some Merits are granted by highly magical and/or technological items.
  • A few are gained in completely unique ways...
