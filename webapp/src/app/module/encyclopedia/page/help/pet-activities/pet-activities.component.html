Poppyopedia » Glossary » Pet Activities

Your pets will engage in many activities on their own. Roughly every hour, a pet will do... SOMETHING.

Possible pet activities are:

  • Hunting - beating up monsters and taking their stuff
  • Gathering - finding food and other resources in the wild
  • Fishing - similar to gathering
  • Crafting - turning materials into tools
  • Hang out with friends, band-mates, significant others, etc
  • Other things, with the right Merit

What a pet decides to do is based on several factors:

  • A pet that's more skilled at a particular activity is more likely to perform that activity
  • A pet that's hungry is more likely to go gathering or fishing
  • If your house is very full, your pets will not perform activities that would bring more items into the house

There are a handful of other actions a pet might engage in, depending on which tool their holding (maps, in particular, may send pets on special adventures), or how they're feeling: for example if you've fed a pet too much junk food and alcohol, it may vomit!
