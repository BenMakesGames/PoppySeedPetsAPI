Poppyopedia » Glossary » ★Kindred

★Kindred is a game you play with your pets.

Your pets play the role of immortal heroes - called "star kindred" - in epic, fantasy adventures; you play the role of story-teller, reading journal entries that describe your pets' progress, and doling out rewards.

Mission Types

  • Hunting - During their travels, the brave adventurers hunt for food! Skilled hunting pets will receive more loot.
  • Wandering Monster - A terrible foe stands in the adventurers' path! Strong fighter pets will receive more loot.
  • Treasure Hunt - Mysterious treasures can be found in the land of the star kindred. Intelligent, perceptive pets will receive more loot.
  • Recruit - The adventurers may occasionally recruit a new member to their party (represented by a plushy, or figurine).
  • Mine Gold - Even in the face of danger, it seems adventuring heroes cannot resist gold. Pets with great strength and endurance will receive more loot.
  • Collect Stone - Gold is neat, but, you know... so are rocks, I guess? Pets with great strength and endurance will receive more loot.
  • Narrative - An important event in the story takes place. (There's a lot of reading for these, and that's about it!)
