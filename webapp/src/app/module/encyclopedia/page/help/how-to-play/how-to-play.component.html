This game is intended to be played at a casual pace.

Roughly every hour, your pets will take actions on their own. They might go hunting for monsters, make a fishing rod using wood lying around the house, hang out with each other; they might even... KISS?!?!

You can see what your pets have been doing in two main ways:

  1. An item's description will tell you how the item was acquired. For example, if Blackberries show up in your house, they were probably gathered by a pet! Click/touch an item to see how it got there.
  2. You can see the activity logs of any of your pets. Click/touch a pet to see what it's been up to.

While you're waiting for your pets to do stuff, there's a few things you can do:

  • Pet or praise your pets to improve their mood and Affection for you.
  • Cook foods for your pets, and either leave them around the house for later, or hand-feed them to your pets to show them you love them!
  • Give your pets tools to improve their chances of successfully performing certain activities (for example, a fishing rod will help a pet fish).
  • Collect items for the Museum.
  • Not play! Yeah, that's right, I'm not afraid to say it! This game is intended to be played at a casual pace. Come back in a few hours, and see what your pets have been up to!
