Poppyopedia » Glossary » Inventory Limits

Players have a "soft" maximum of 100 items in their house.

There are various ways you can acquire items above this soft maximum, and there is currently no hard maximum, HOWEVER: when your house is over-full, your pets will only craft items; they will no longer go fishing, hunting, gathering, or any other activity that might further increase the number of items in the house.

To reduce the number of items in your house, you can put items in your pets' lunchboxes, donate items to the museum, or, if there's nothing better to do with them, simply recycle them. (Even items worth no recycling points can be "recycled".)

There is currently no way to increase this maximum.
