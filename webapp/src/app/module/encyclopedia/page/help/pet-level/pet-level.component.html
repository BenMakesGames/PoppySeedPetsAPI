Poppyopedia » Glossary » Pet Levels, & Gaining Experience

Pets have various skills: brawl, crafts, arcana, music, and several others.

When a pet's skills are tested - for example, when it goes out adventuring, and encounters a monster - the pet will gain some experience points.

If all of a pet's needs aren't met, it will gain fewer experience; every unmet need reduces the experience gain. A pet that's poorly taken care of might not gain any experience points at all!

When a pet gains enough experience points, it will "level up"! When a pet levels up, one of the skills which was just tested will be improved.

As an example, if your pet gains experience points while sneaking up on a Scarecrow to beat it up, and happens to level up from doing so, then the pet will increase either its brawl or stealth (since those were the skills involved in the encounter).

Another way to look at a pet's level is that it's the sum total of all of its skills.
