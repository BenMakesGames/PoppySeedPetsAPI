Poppyopedia » Glossary » Lunchboxes

Putting food into your pets' lunchboxes has two advantages:

  1. When hungry, pets are much more likely to eat food out of their lunchboxes rather than eating whatever they find when out hunting and gathering! This gives them more time to do other things, AND allows you to provide them more-filling foods with flavors they really like.

  2. Because you may have your own plans for items lying around the house, pets will never eat these. Lunchboxes allow you to pick which foods a pet can eat (and which they can't!)
