Poppyopedia » Glossary » Guilds

Guilds are similar to Groups - pets can join them, and participate in unique activities as members.

How pets join a Guild is something of a mystery...

The Nine Guilds

  • Time's Arrow - digital archaeologists that use time-bending glitches to uncover old data
  • Light and Shadow - they speak for and defend spirits, when no one else will
  • Tapestries - they seek to understand the fabric of the universe, and repair any damage it may suffer
  • Inner Sanctum - philosopher-monks who wish to master mind and body
  • Dwarfcraft - mad scientists and inventors who travel Project-E looking for strange materials to craft with
  • Gizubi's Garden - gardeners, cooks, and hedonists who believe in preserving - and enjoying - life, and everything it produces
  • High Impact - thrill-seekers who look for new challenges, and use SCIENCE to win the day
  • The Universe Forgets - they believe the universe is on the cusp of rebirth, and wish to help it transition smoothly
  • Correspondence - adventurers and couriers; they ensure that messages reach their destination without fail
