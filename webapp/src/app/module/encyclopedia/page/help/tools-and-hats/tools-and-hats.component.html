Poppyopedia » Glossary » Tools & Hats

Tools

Pets can hold tools to make them more-effective at certain tasks, and learn faster.

Tools never break (even Stereotypical Torches... which is weird, when you really think about it...)

Most tools are crafted by pets, but a few can be found in other ways.

Hats

Hats can be worn by pets once they acquire a special Merit.

Hats confer no effects or bonuses; they just look cute.
